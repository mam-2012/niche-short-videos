OpenClaw Publisher

OpenClaw Publisher is a personal automation tool that uploads short-form vertical videos generated by an internal pipeline to its operator's own social media accounts (YouTube Shorts and TikTok). It is operated by a single individual for personal use only and is not offered to third parties.

How it works

  1. The operator's local pipeline generates a vertical video.
  2. The video is uploaded to the operator's private storage.
  3. The operator reviews the video in a private Discord channel and approves or rejects it.
  4. On approval, OpenClaw Publisher uploads the video to the operator's own TikTok and YouTube accounts using the official APIs.
