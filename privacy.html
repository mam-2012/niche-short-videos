Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-09

Discord TikTok Publisher ("the Application") allows users to automatically publish generated short-form videos to their own TikTok accounts after approving them in Discord. This policy explains what data the Application collects and how it is used.

1. Data we collect

  • TikTok OAuth tokens issued by TikTok when a user connects their TikTok account. These tokens are used solely to publish videos to that same user's TikTok account on their behalf.
  • Discord account identifiers needed to deliver generated videos to the user's Discord channel and to receive their approval or rejection.
  • Generated video files and associated metadata (titles, descriptions, hashtags) produced by the Application for the user.

The Application does not collect personal data beyond what is required to provide this functionality, and never accesses content belonging to any account other than the one the user has explicitly connected.

2. How we store data

OAuth tokens and account identifiers are stored securely and are transmitted only to the official API endpoints of the platforms they authorize (TikTok and Discord). Generated video files are retained only as long as needed to deliver them for review and publish them on approval.

3. Data sharing

The Application does not sell or share user data with third parties. It communicates only with the official APIs of TikTok and Discord, each acting on behalf of the account the user has connected.

4. Data retention and deletion

Users may disconnect their TikTok account at any time, which revokes the Application's tokens and deletes them from our storage. Users may also revoke access directly at:

  • TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/setting/apps-and-website

5. Security

OAuth flows use the official endpoints of each provider over HTTPS. Tokens are stored with restricted access and are used only to perform actions the user has explicitly approved through Discord.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The most recent version is always available on this page.
